Welcome to Chaitanya agro-tourism and Adventure Park

Find adventure and relaxation at Chaitanya

Welcome to Chaitanya Agro-Tourism and Adventure Park! We are excited to have you join us for an unforgettable experience. Our park is a beautiful and serene location nestled in the heart of nature, where you can escape the hustle and bustle of city life and reconnect with the great outdoors. We offer a wide range of activities and adventures for all ages, including zip lining, ropes courses, farm tours, and nature walks. But what truly sets us apart is our commitment to sustainability and preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. We strive to provide an authentic and immersive experience for all of our guests, and we can't wait for you to create lasting memories with us at Chaitanya. So come and explore all that our park has to offer, and we hope you have a wonderful time with us!
