# Know about us

Hello and welcome to Chaitanya Agricultural Tourism Farm and Adventure Park!

At Chaitanya Agricultural Tourism Farm, we offer a unique farm tour experience where you can learn about traditional farming practices and interact with the animals on our farm. You can also participate in a hurda party, where you can roast and enjoy fresh corn picked straight from the fields. If you're in the mood to cool off, we also have a swimming pool where you can take a dip and enjoy a refreshing swim. And when the weather is warm, you can dance in the rain with our rain dance activity.

n addition to these fun and exciting activities, we also offer a range of options for fishing, boating, and camping. And when you're ready to take a break, be sure to try out our delicious dining options.

So come and visit us at Chaitanya Agricultural Tourism Farm and Adventure Park for a memorable and enjoyable day in the great outdoors.
